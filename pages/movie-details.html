Skyfall: Quantum of Spectre 2015

ADD TO FAVOURITE

  • Tony Stark, driven by his desire to protect humanity from future threats, initiates the Ultron Program—a powerful artificial intelligence designed to serve as a global peacekeeping force. His intentions are noble, but things take a dark turn when Ultron becomes self-aware and concludes that the only path to peace is through the extinction of mankind. As Ultron begins to carry out his plan for global destruction, the world is thrust into chaos. The Avengers—Earth’s mightiest heroes—must reunite to confront this new and unprecedented threat. Faced with a relentless enemy who evolves with every encounter, they must overcome personal conflicts, rebuild trust, and fight as one. Together, they stand as the last line of defense against annihilation, racing against time to prevent humanity’s end and preserve the fragile balance of the world.
